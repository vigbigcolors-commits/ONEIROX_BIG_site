Science · not superstition

Chronobiology, not astrology

Cajochen and colleagues measured sleep in a controlled laboratory — no windows, no clocks — and still found lunar-phase effects on deep sleep and REM. The mechanism is not mystical. Light exposure, circadian timing, and melatonin dynamics interact with sleep architecture. Oneirox uses lunar phase as context for dream intensity, the same way we use somatic data from the Mapper: another layer of honest signal.

This calculator does not predict your future. It reads what the sleeping brain was likely working with on a specific night — then you take that context into Decode for a mechanism-based reading.
